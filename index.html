Step into Artisans Collective, where creativity thrives amidst a welcoming community. Here, we celebrate the remarkable work of local artisans and craftsmen, offering a curated marketplace brimming with their passion-fueled creations. From intricately designed jewelry to hand-thrown pottery and beyond, each piece tells a story of dedication and artistry. Whether you're an artisan seeking a platform to showcase your talent or a discerning shopper in search of unique treasures, Artisans Collective invites you to explore and support the artisans behind the craft.

Our founder

Artisans Collective was founded by Essec Business Student, Jia Kalra. She aimed to build a platform where customers looking for high quality, one of a kind products would be able to come into contact with local artisans. Following the rise of mass manufacturing and large corporation, arts that have been around for centuries are being forgotten and artisans are being forced to give up their crafts. Artisans Collective hopes to revive these crafts by offering convenience to customers.
